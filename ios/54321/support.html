54321 Breath: Support

Welcome to the support page for 54321 Breath, the app that helps you find calm through guided 5-4-3-2-1 grounding exercises.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the 5-4-3-2-1 grounding technique?
A: The 5-4-3-2-1 grounding technique is an evidence-based mindfulness exercise that helps you reconnect with your senses and the present moment. It involves identifying:
  • 5 things you can see
  • 4 things you can touch
  • 3 things you can hear
  • 2 things you can smell
  • 1 thing you can taste
This technique is widely used by therapists and mental health professionals to help manage anxiety, stress, and panic attacks.
Q: Do I need an internet connection to use the app?
A: No, 54321 Breath works completely offline. All features, including background music and sound effects, are available without an internet connection. This makes it perfect for use anywhere, anytime you need grounding support.
Q: Can I save my progress or sessions?
A: The app is designed to be used fresh each time, focusing on the present moment. We don't save session data to keep the experience simple and privacy-focused. Each time you use the app, you start a new grounding exercise.
Q: How do I control the background music?
A: Tap the mute button in the top-left corner of the screen to toggle background music on or off. The setting is remembered for your next session.
Q: What if I can't find items for a particular sense?
A: That's okay! The app provides helpful suggestions, but you can also identify items on your own. If you're having trouble with a particular sense (like smell or taste), you can tap "I found all items, let's continue" to proceed. The important thing is to engage with your senses and stay present.
Q: Is this app a replacement for therapy or professional mental health care?
A: No. While 54321 Breath is a helpful tool for managing stress and anxiety, it is not a replacement for professional mental health care. If you're experiencing severe anxiety, depression, or other mental health concerns, please consult with a qualified healthcare provider or therapist.
Q: Does the app collect my data?
A: No. 54321 Breath does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app operates entirely on your device. We don't track usage, require accounts, or share information with third parties. Your privacy is fully protected. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
Q: Can I use this app multiple times a day?
A: Absolutely! You can use 54321 Breath as often as you need. Many people find it helpful to practice grounding exercises multiple times throughout the day, especially during stressful moments or as part of a daily mindfulness routine.
