Chord to Music: Support

Welcome to the support page for Chord to Music, the app that helps you explore chord progressions based on your chosen key and mood.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I save progressions?

A: Currently, you can copy or screenshot your favorite progressions.

Q: Do I need an internet connection?

A: No, all chord progressions are generated directly on your device.
